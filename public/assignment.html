Now that you have chosen your final project ideas, the next step is to create an idea brief for each of your projects using Google Docs.

This brief should be thorough enough to allow us to understand your vision for the final product. This is crucial for the next stages of design and development. Please follow these guidelines while creating your idea brief:

  • Project Brief Description: Write a concise and clear description of your project. This should include what the project is about, its purpose, the problem it is trying to solve, and how it intends to solve it.
  • Target Audience: Identify the general group of people who are intended to use your application. While a user persona is not required at this stage, you should at least know the age range, occupations, and other general characteristics of your prospective users.
